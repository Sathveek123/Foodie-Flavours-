## 🚀 Overview

Foodie Flavors is a premium restaurant and food ordering platform built to showcase culinary experiences through modern design, engaging food presentations, and seamless customer interactions.

The website combines elegant visuals, intuitive navigation, and responsive performance to enhance online food discovery and ordering.
